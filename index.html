Delete Your Account

This page explains how users of the Lead Management App can request the deletion of their account and associated data.

How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your account permanently, please send an email to:

Email: levalegowtham3@gmail.com

Your email must include:

  • Your registered name
  • Your registered email or phone
  • A clear request stating: “I want to delete my account.”

What Data Will Be Deleted

After confirming your identity, the following data will be removed permanently:

  • User profile information
  • Leads and client details added by the user
  • Notes, tasks, or follow-up history
  • App settings and login sessions

Data That May Be Retained

Some data may be retained temporarily for:

  • Legal obligations
  • Fraud prevention
  • Security requirements

Retention period (if applicable): up to 30 days.

Processing Time

Your deletion request will be processed within up to 7 days.
